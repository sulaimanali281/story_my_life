ITSRobocon

    ITSRobocon is part of robotics team from institut technology sepuluh nopember. This team created to join abu robocon asia pasific. In the competition robots compete to complete a task within a set period of time. The team must build own robot by theme that determine by committee. Every year has many deferent themes of task compettion. 

    I joined this team as Co-leader and the electrical-programming division. I have responsibility for research, design electronic, troubleshooting, and programming microcontroller.

    Due to the influence of COVID-19, competition in 2020 was held by sending videos to every country that was selected. I as a part of this team won the highest award FIRST PLACE of ABU Robocon Indonesia Competition 2020. So, my team become representative of indonesian team to join festival ABU Robocon Asia-Pacific. This competition have Theme “ROBO RUGBY 7s”. Every team must make robot that can be play rugby 7’s. The game using two robots and five obstacles as five defending players. The highlight of this game is how the two robots collaborate to score Try and the Goal Kick. The main and unique challenge of this game will be Goal Kick, kicking the Kick Ball over the cross bar of the conversion post because of the unique shape of the rugby ball.
