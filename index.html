Internship

PT Jasa Marga IoT Lab (Agust 2020 - Sept 2020)

In this internship, I learn Single-Lane Free Flow (SLFF)  wich is one of the projects undertaken by Jasa Marga IoT Lab. SLFF is an electronic transaction system, by using this payment method it hoped that road users don’t need to stop their vehicles at toll gates. This developing system is RFID sticker based system. The function of this RFID is to be the identity of road users. Whereas, for the payment process is using mobile app named “ flo “. So that road users can move gradually, this system is created by combining the e-toll and “flo” systems. In the SLFF development process, it requires an additional system that can be classified the e-toll users and flo users it’s namely a system of classification and queuing order of vehicles called pre-detection.

Kontes Robot Tematik Indonesia 2020

KRTMI 2020 also online competition. I as a part ofRIVAL ITS get award: Practical and Safe Winner for Thematic Robot Indonesia Competition 2020. In this event, we design a robot that functions to lift the patient with the minimum possible physical contact between the nurse and the patient and the minimum possible movement of the patient. The patient is assumed to be in a supine sleeping condition. The robot, control by the nurse, moves closer to the bed, extends its arm, grabs and lifts the patient from the bed. Nurses are not allowed to use assistive devices in the form of sticks or the like to move/lift patients. 
This competition has a mission to help deal with the problem of covid 19, that physical contact between patient and medical personnel, so spreading covid 19 can be reduced.

LAI2-COVID19 Sub Robot, Ditmawa ITS

LAI2-COVID19 held by ditmawa its. I and 2 my friend vian putri join this competition as KUYBOT Team.  We create innovative medical service robots design for handling covid 19 patients.The robot's name is Hosiro-Usiro. HOSIRO (Hospital System Robot) is a robotic system specifically for handling COVID19 in hospitals. HOSIRO has a service function in terms of delivery, is able to see the patient's physical condition in the form of body temperature and heart rate, and can be used by patients as a medium for interacting. Meanwhile, USIRO (Universal System Robot) is a robot that has a service function for self-quarantined patients. USIRO's service functions can be in the form of interacting with other people, buying/picking up food from online motorcycle taxis and so on. USIRO is equipped with a treadmill as a walking control of the robot. Through robot control using a treadmill, patients who are self-isolating can feel virtual social so that they can reduce the patient's stress load during self-quarantine.
